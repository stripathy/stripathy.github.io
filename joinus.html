Join Us

Postdoctoral fellowships:

  • Applicants should apply by directly contacting Shreejoy with a CV and short cover letter outlining their interests. The ideal applicant would have a strong background in data analysis and neuroscience and/or genomics (or a strong desire to learn). Experience closely working with experimentalists is a plus. Teaching or advising experience is also a plus.

Graduate students:

  • We are currently looking for Masters students, with the option to later transition to a PhD. Interested students can apply through the Institute of Medical Science and should coordinate their application with Shreejoy by email (shreejoy.tripathy@utoronto.ca). The ideal student would have some familiarity with programming and data analysis or have a strong desire to learn. Experience collecting experimental data in neuroscience or molecular biology is also a plus.
  • Shreejoy is also available to serve on the thesis committees of graduate students at the University of Toronto.

Undergraduate students:

  • We are also looking for undergraduate students with some experience in programming and data analysis. Interested students should contact Shreejoy with a few sentences outlining their interests and a list of relevant coursework. Diverse candidates with non-traditional backgrounds are especially encouraged to apply.
